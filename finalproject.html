Final Project!

Below you will see the game my team and I created for the CSC 106 final project. It is a simple 2D platformer created via khan's programming environemnt. You start out selecting a character, then with your mouse selecting the start game button. You then move through levels with the arrow keys whilst jumping over obstacles to collect letters. Once you've spelt out the word from collceting the letters, you move on to the next level. Beat all the levels to win, but most of all have fun!

Final Project!

Below you will see the game my team and I created for the CSC 106 final project. It is a simple 2D platformer created via khan's programming environemnt. You start out by selecting a character with your mouse, then the start game button. You move through levels with the arrow keys whilst jumping over obstacles to collect letters. Once you've spelled out the word from collceting the letters, you move on to the next level. Beat all the levels to win, but most of all have fun!
